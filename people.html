Arjun Sathyan Kulathuvayal
PhD student

Arjun obtained his B.S. and MS in Physics from the Pondicherry University, Puducherry (India). The research activities of Arjun mainly related to condensed matter Physics (CMP), wherein especially the first principle analysis (DFT) and experimental methodologies on materials under extreme circumstances. The research interests are further extended to 2-D thin films and spintronics. Arjun had also worked as an intern in the field of 2D thin films at the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Trivandrum (India). His Master's specialization is in CMP, and thesis is entitled "Synthesis and characterization of Strontium doped Copper ferrite nanoparticles - DFT & experimental analysis".
